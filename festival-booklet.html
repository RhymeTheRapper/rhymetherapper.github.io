Film Booklet

New York City Film Festival 2022 Booklet

This was designed using the content from the New York City Film Festival. I wanted to reconfigure how a booklet is used and make it more portable and compact. I designed it in a way that the outer panels could be ripped off and discarded, while the inner panels would be kept. The inner panels included essential information like the calendar. On the first page, the outer panel includes a ticket, in practice this would mean that the booklet is the ticket for the festival making it multipurpose. A attendee would buy the booklet and get their ticket and all information in one package. The first page also includes a sticker sheet where attendees could peel of the stickers and spread them around the city. I designed the cover of the booklet to reflect this, showing a way that the stickers could be used.
